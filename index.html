How the math works. Indicators (VWAP / RSI-14 / EMA-20-50-200 / ATR-14) are computed in your browser from FMP intraday bars on the selected timeframe, so they don't burn extra API calls. VWAP is session-based (resets each day). RVOL. When at least 2 prior days of intraday bars are available, RVOL is time-adjusted (tag TA): today's cumulative volume up to the current time of day ÷ the typical cumulative volume by that same time of day across prior days — so a pre-market or early-session read isn't understated. If there isn't enough intraday history it falls back to the daily proxy (tag D: day volume ÷ 30-day average). Float comes from FMP's shares-float endpoint; if your plan doesn't include it, float shows ?.
