Free-plan data sourcing. FMP's free plan blocks /quote for most micro-caps ("Premium Endpoint"), so price, day range and volume come from Alpaca (free IEX, covers all symbols), while float, country and average volume come from FMP /profile. Each proxy column is tagged ~:
• Range = where price sits in today's high–low band (Alpaca); upper half (≥50%) is the “above-VWAP” proxy.
• RVOL = today's Alpaca volume ÷ FMP 30-day average volume.
• Mom = up on the day (proxy for RSI>50).
• Trend shows ? on the free plan — 50/200-day averages need FMP's Premium /quote. So alerts use the other 5 checks; Pass = x/5.
• Float from shares-float; Country from company profile.
Note on Alpaca IEX volume: IEX is ~2–3% of market volume, so RVOL on very thin names is approximate — treat it as a relative signal, not an exact multiple.
Trade plan (long-only). Entry = last price. Stop = today's low (or −3% if low ≥ price). R = entry−stop. TP1/TP2/TP3 = entry +1R/+2R/+3R. Mechanical levels, not advice — confirm and size yourself.
